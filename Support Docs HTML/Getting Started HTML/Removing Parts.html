  • Remove Parts from Print Bed

 

WARNING: Always pry with the scraper in a direction away from your body. The part may separate from the bed quickly and unexpectedly.  Failing to scrape away from your body may result in injury.

 

Removing Parts from the Print Bed

Supplies Needed: Putty Knife from Accessory Kit

To remove a part from the print bed:

  1. Remove the print bed from the printer.
  2. Rest the print bed securely against a flat surface.
    Note: We recommend resting the bed against the floor or a table.
  3. Keep the angle between the scraper and the print bed small while you are removing your part. Scraping at a large angle may damage your print bed.
  4. Always start scraping your part at a corner and work towards the center to avoid damaging the part.
