Goal:

  • Connect your printer to Eiger

 

 

Connect your Mark Two Printer

Before beginning with setup, if your printer is connected to the internet, please disconnect it.

To connect your printer:

  1. Sign in to your Eiger account

  2. Navigate to the "Settings Page" by either:

    • Using this link: https://www.eiger.io/settings?s=profile
    • Clicking the "Settings Menu Button" in the top right and then selecting "Settings



  3. Navigate to the "Printer Settings" by clicking on "Printer" in the left sidebar.

  4. Click "Add Printer"



  5. In order to register your printer you need to enter the following information:
    • A Name for your Printer
    • The Machine ID and Access Key for your printer
      Note: The Machine ID and Access Key can be found on any of the following:
      • The label on the back of your printer
      • The included welcome card that came with the printer
      • The info screen of your printer



  6. Enter the printer name, Machine ID, and Access Key and click "Add Printer".

  7. Connect your Mark Two to the internet.
