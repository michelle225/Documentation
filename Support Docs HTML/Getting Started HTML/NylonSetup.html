Goals:

  • Install Filament Feed Tube to the Mark Two
  • Install or Remove Feed Tube from Dry Box

  

Caution! 
Make sure the Nylon Dry Box Remains CLOSED and LATCHED at all times except when quickly loading or replacing filament. Do NOT leave Nylon spool exposed to atmosphere.

 

 

 

Install Filament Feed Tube on the Mark Two.

To install the Filament Feed Tube on your printer

  1. Install clear plastic Filament Feed Tube into brass fitting in the black drybox case.



  2. Insert the other end of the Filament Feed Tube into the opening in the back of the Mark Two.




  3. Insert the Filament Feed Tube into the Push-To-Connect fitting at the rear of the Plastic Extruder, and push firmly to secure it.




  4. Gently tug the Feed Tube to make sure that is properly seated in the Extruder fitting.

Installation and Removal of Filament Feed Tube Into Drybox

To remove the Filament Feed Tube from the Drybox fitting:

  1. Push the orange fitting into the brass piece to unlock it
  2. Pull the filament feed tube out from the fitting
    Note: The filament feed tube should come out without any struggle. If the tube will not come out, make sure the fitting is unlocked and try again.

If you don't plan on using your Mark Two for an extended period of time, or you need to travel, move locations, or otherwise disconnect the dry box, make sure to USE THE PROVIDED RED PLUG TO SEAL THE DRY BOX. Failure to do so will damage your nylon and can lead to damaged parts if re-used.
