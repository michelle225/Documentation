Before You Get Started

Every MarkForged printer should be delivered with the correct printer kit type (Standard, Professional, or Enterprise) installed. However, there may be times when the printer kit type may need to be reinstalled to the printer. Taking either of the options listed below should update your printer to the kit type that you've paid for. If you encounter any issues with this process, please contact support.

Automatic Load

To automatically load the kit type to your Mark Two printer:

  1. Restart your printer
  2. Connect your printer to our servers via WiFi or Ethernet.

Manual Load (USB)

Manually download a kit upgrade .mfp file and execute it on the printer.

  1. Login to Eiger
  2. Click on "Settings" from the menu at the top right of your screen.
  3. Select your printer from the list of printers under the "Printers" heading on the left side of your screen.
  4. Click on the "..." button near the top of your screen.
  5. Select "Download Kit Upgrade".



  6. Download the .mfp file page in Eiger and transfer it to a USB thumb drive.
  7. Insert the USB drive into your printer and run the .mfp file.
