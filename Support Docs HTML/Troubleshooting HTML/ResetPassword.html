Resetting Your Password

If you have forgotten your password or are unable to login you may reset your password.

To reset your password:

  1. Navigate to https://www.eiger.io/signin



  2. Click On the "Forgot?" Button in the "Password Input Field"



  3. Enter your email address.
    This must be the same email address that is associated with your account.



  4. We will send a "Password Reset Link" to the email address you provided. Once you have received this email follow this link.



  5. Enter your new password and then click "Reset Password"
