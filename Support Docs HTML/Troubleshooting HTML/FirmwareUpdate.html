  2. Once the machine has been reset to factory defaults, use another USB stick to install the update as shown below.

Updating Firmware via USB

Your Mark Two printer can be updated via USB. You will need a different USB thumb drive than the one that came with your printer.

To update your Mark Two Firmware via USB:

  1. Log into your Eiger.io account and navigate to https://www.eiger.io/settings/about.
  2. Download the USB Update
  3. Put the USB update on a USB drive.
    Note: Do NOT use the MarkForged USB Backup drive that came with your printer. The MarkForged USB drive that came with your printer is a re-flash drive and it will perform a factory reset on your printer.
  4. Plug the USB drive with the update into the slot in the back of your printer.
    Note: Once the USB drive is plugged in, you should see a green notification appear at the top of the screen.
  5. Begin the update process by either:
    • Pressing the "Update Available" in the green oval at the top of your screen.
    • Navigating to the "Settings Page" and press "Update Manager".



  6. Press “Update” to apply the update. The update will take a few minutes to install and your printer will restart at the end. Do not turn your printer off during the update process.

Updating Firmware via Wi-Fi

Your Mark Two printer can be updated via Wi-Fi. Before beginning, make sure your printer is connected to a Wi-Fi network. For more information, see Connecting to Wi-Fi, below.

To update your Mark Two Firmware via Wi-Fi:

  1. Select "Settings" from the menu at the upper right corner of your printer's LCD screen.
  2. Select "Update Manager" from the list of options.



  3. Press the "Cloud Update" icon if it is available. The update will take a few minutes to install and your printer will restart at the end. Do not turn your printer off during the update process.
    Note: An update is available if the "Cloud Update" icon is blue. If the icon is gray, your system is either up to date or not connected to the internet.

Connecting to Wi-Fi

To connect your Mark Two to a Wi-Fi network:

  1. Select the "Wi-Fi" icon. If you are offline, the "Offline" icon will be highlighted. If you are connected, the icon for your means of connectivity will be highlighted.



  2. Select the "Wi-Fi" icon and then select “Configure” to set up your WiFi network connection.



  3. Select your Wi-Fi network from the menu or enter the name of your WiFi network. If required, enter in your WiFi network password.
  4. Press “Save” when completed. The Wi-Fi connection may take a minute to authenticate.
