Goal:

  • Save Print Logs to USB

Important:

Do NOT use the USB flash drive provided with your Mark One Composite 3D Printer. The supplied drive is needed in the event you machine needs to be factory reset.

Saving Print Logs to a USB Thumb Drive

In the event that the MarkForged Support Team needs to see the logs and errors from your machine, you can copy the requested log files to a USB thumb drive.

To save logs to a USB thumb drive:

  1. Insert a FAT32 formatted USB drive into the back of your Mark Two Printer
  2. Press the Menu button in the upper right corner of the printer's LCD screen.



  3. Select "USB/On Board Storage" from the list of menu options.



  4. Press "Save Logs to USB" and wait for the operation to complete.



  5. Go back to the Home screen, and then remove your USB Drive.
