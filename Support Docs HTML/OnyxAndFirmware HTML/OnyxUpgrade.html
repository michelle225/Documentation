Before Getting Started

Before you get started, there are some supplies you will need to complete this upgrade.

Supplied Needed:

  • Mark Two Enterprise Printer
  • Onyx Upgrade Kit (includes one extruder and one 3mm hex key)
  • USB flash drive
  • Airtight container large enough to fit a Markforged FFF spool
  • Paper towel
  • Internet access

Downloading Onyx Firmware

To download the firmware for Onyx:

  1. Open and login to Eiger.
  2. Click on the Settings Menu in the upper right corner of the screen.



  3. Select "About Eiger" from the list of menu options.



  4. Click on the "Download Firmware for Onyx" button.



  5. Transfer the file onto a USB Flash Drive.

Replacing the Extruder

To print with Onyx, you will need to replace the extruder that is currently in your printer with one made for Onyx. Failure to do so will result in damaged components and other negative outcomes.

To replace the extruder on your Mark Two printer:

  1. Run the "Unload Nylon" routine from your printer's LCD touchscreen menu.
  2. Remove the Nylon spool from the dry box and store it in an airtight container with desiccant.
  3. Unscrew the Fiber Bowden Tube thumbscrew and disconnect the feeder tube from the black push-to-connect fitting.



  4. Turn the printer off.
  5. Disconnect the extruder motor cable harness.



  6. Use the provided 3mm hex key to loosen the two screws that clamp the extruder to its bracket.



  7. Remove the extruder fro the printer.
  8. Use a paper towel to wipe away any excess thermal compound that may be on the extruder, bracket, or tubing.
  9. Remove the brass fitting and bracket screws from the extruder.



  10. Remove the included Onyx Upgrade extruder from its packaging. Screw the brass fitting and bracket screws into the Upgrade Extruder.



  11. Place the Upgrade Extruder on the extruder bracket in the printer and tighen the bracket screws using the 3mm Allen key until the extruder is securely clamped to the bracket.
  12. Reconnect the extruder motor cable harness and ensure that the wires are folded neatly behind the extruder.



  13. Reconnect the Fiber Bowden Tube and feeder tube to the extruder.
  14. Insert the USB Flash Drive containing the Onyx firmare update into the back ot he printer and turn the printer on.
  15. Select the upgrade icon at the top of the printer's LCD touchscreen.
  16. Select "USB Update" from the options on the screen.
  17. Allow the printer to update and restart.

Once your printer has updated and restarted, it will be ready to print with Onyx.
