Important:

You must be your Organization's Administrator in order to rename a printer.

  

Renaming a Printer in your Organization

To rename a printer in your organization:

  1. If your printer is connected to the internet please disconnect it.
  2. Sign in to your Eiger account.
  3. Navigate to the "Settings Page"by either:
    • Navigating to https://www.eiger.io/settings/profile.
    • Clicking the "Settings Menu" button at the top right of the screen and then selecting "Settings".



  4. Navigate to the "Printer Settings" by clicking on "Printer" in the left sidebar.



  5. Select the printer that you wish to rename from the list of printers registered with your organization. 



  6. Enter the new name for your printer and click "Update Name".



  7. Re-connect your printer to the internet.

Once it has connected it will appear in you Eiger account with the updated name.
