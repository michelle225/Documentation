Important:

You must be your Organization's Administrator in order to invite new users to join your Organization.

Learn What Users Can Do in Eiger

Users in your Organization CAN:

  • Upload, view, and clone models
  • Create and view prints
  • Create and send print jobs to registered printers in your organization

Users in your Organization CANNOT:

  • Invite new users to your organization
  • Remove users from your organization
  • Delete or modify other users' models or prints
  • Register new printers to your organization

Invite Users to your Eiger Organization

To invite users to your Eiger organization:

  1. Sign in to your Eiger account
  2. Navigate to the "Settings Page" by either:
    • Navigating to https://www.eiger.io/settings/profile
    • Or clicking on theÂ "Settings Menu" button and then clicking on "Settings".



  3. Navigate to the "Organization Settings" by clicking on "Organization" in the left sidebar.



  4. Enter the email address of the user you would like to invite in the input field.



  5. Click Send Invite.

The user should receive an invitation email shortly.
