Changing your Organization's Administrator

Changing your organization's administrator can only be done by the current Administrator and each organization can have only one Administrator

MarkForged will not change the administrator account for you. If an administrator is no longer with your organization, please work with your IT team to recover that user's email address and reset the user's Eiger password.

To change your organization's administrator:

  • Open Eiger and sign in to your account.
  • Select the menu icon in the upper right corner of the screen.



  • Select "Settings" from the list of menu options.



  • Select "Organization" from the list of menu options on the left side of the screen.



  • Click the "Transfer Ownership" button.
  • Input the new organization owner.
  • Enter your current password.
  • Select the user from the drop-down list of users.
  • Press the "Transfer" button.
